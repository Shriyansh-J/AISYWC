Mysuru (Mysore), The most enchanting city in South India. A city that is famous for its glittering royal heritage palaces, magnificent monuments, and marvelous architectural structures. A World Heritage place Mysuru has a royal history behind its richness in culture and tradition. It served as a capital city of the Kingdom of Mysore for nearly six centuries. The Kingdom was ruled by Wadiyar Dynasty.

The city is situated in the foothills of the Chamundi Hills, which is about 146kms (91mi) southwest of Bengaluru. The city is well-connected transport system with numerous options to get around Mysore. Public transport is the most efficient mean of transport to travel within and outside the city. Cab facility is active and easily available(Uber/Ola).

Mysore is home to rich spices, silk, and sandalwood. Mysore has historical importance and houses the opulent Mysore Palace. Other destinations you should spare your time for are - Chamundeshwari Temple, Brindavan Gardens, Jaganmohan Palace, St. Philomena's Cathedral and Krishna Raj Sagara to name a few.
