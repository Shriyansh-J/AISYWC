About our Host City

Mysuru

Mysuru (Mysore), one of the main cities of Karnataka is located in the foothills of the Chamundi Hills about 146 km (91 mi) southwest of Bangalore. A well-connected transport system lets you travel hassle-free both within and outside the city. You’ll find numerous options to get around Mysore with cabs (Uber/Ola cabs are active and easily available), the local bus systems being the most efficient.

Mysore is home to rich spices,silk and sandalwood.Noted for its heritage structures and palaces, Mysore has historical importance and houses the opulent Mysore Palace. Other destinations you should spare your time for are- Chamundeshwari Temple,Brindavan Gardens,Jaganmohan Palace,St. Philomena's Cathedral and Krishna Raj Sagara to name a few.
