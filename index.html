About AISYWC

All India Student - Young Professionals - Women in Engineering Congress (AISYWC) is the annual flagship event of IEEE India Council. AISYWC is conceived as a congregation of inventors, professionals, entrepreneurs, visionaries and some of the greatest minds in the country in order to provide wider and diversified platform to hear domain experts and peers, to share best practices and lessons learnt, latest research, new development initiatives, innovations, and first hand experiences regarding prototyping and initial service offerings in various fields of Technology. By bringing these amazing people from all walks of life, the AISYWC empowers its participants to Aspire, Ideate, SYnchronize, Widen Thoughts and Capture the Goal. This will be the largest congress in India in its kind with expected delegates of about 500.
